What We Do

diaas partners with the world's leading businesses to build, manage, and scale high-performing Global Capability Centers (GCCs) that accelerate innovation, enable transformation, solve complex challenges, and drive operational efficiency through fully integrated, in-house teams.

Our end-to-end solutions simplify global operations by seamlessly integrating workforce, HR, workspace, and compliance across premier talent hubs. As a market leader in GCC establishment, diaas blends proven expertise with technology-driven innovation. Our AI-powered 1Wrk global work platform unifies people, processes, and infrastructure into a single, cohesive solution—enabling enterprises to access world-class talent and drive sustainable growth and success.
